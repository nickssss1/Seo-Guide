Understanding Indexing

Indexing is how search engines find, analyze, and store your web pages so they can appear in search results. If your pages aren’t indexed, they won’t show up, no matter how great your content is.

Challenges in Indexing

When I first started, many of my pages weren’t appearing in search results. I learned that proper sitemaps, robots.txt configuration, and internal linking are crucial. Plus, tools like Linkdexing and SmartIndexer can help ensure orphaned pages are crawled and indexed (BHW, 2024).
