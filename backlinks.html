The Importance of Backlinks

Backlinks signal trust and authority to search engines. Initially, I thought any backlink would help, but quality is more important than quantity. Relevant, authoritative links from niche-related sites are key.

Building High-Quality Backlinks

I focused on guest blogging, influencer partnerships, and creating shareable content like infographics. Ensuring anchor text diversity and contextual relevance is crucial to avoiding penalties and appearing natural to search engines.
